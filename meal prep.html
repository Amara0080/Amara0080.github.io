Top Foods, Ideas, and Tips to Help With Meal Preps

Ever wanted to start meal prepping your food, but didn't know exactly where to start? That's no worry! Here, we'll list numerous foods, ideas, and tips that'll help you along the way. Prepping meals for the week is the perfect way to stay on track of your healthy eating plan.

Top 20 Foods:

1. Hard-Boiled Eggs
2. Quinoa
3. Brown Rice
4. Overnight Oats
5. Roasted Veggies
6. Iced Tea
7. Ground Meat (Lean ground beef or turkey)
8. Grilled Chicken Breasts
9. Whole Wheat Pasta
10. Trail Mix
11. Grilled Shrimp
12. Sauteed Kale
13. Spaghetti Squash
14. Sliced Veggies & Hummis
15. Grilled Lamb Chops
16. Lentils
17. Baked Sweet Potatoes
18. Wild-Caught Salmon
19. Cauliflower Mash
20. Roasted Chickpeas

Meal Prep Ideas Based on Time of Day:

Breakfast:

1. Instant oatmeal with berries or chopped up apples and cinnamon
2. Freezer-friendly breakfast taquitos
3. Whole-wheat English muffin with peanut butter and sliced banana
4. Make-ahead mini breakfast pizzas
5. Scrambled (or hard-boiled) eggs with cheese and spinach
6. Overnight Oats Jars
7. Freezer-friendly protein pancakes
8. Make ahead egg white crustless quiche with mixed veggies (usually spinach and red pepper)
9. Granola yogurt parfait

Lunch:

1. Instant Noodle Soup Jars
2. Some sort of pasta meal prep
3. Coconut mango chicken meal prep bowls
4. Sweet chili chicken meal prep bowls
5. Lettuce wraps with leftover protein from dinner
6. Kale & quinoa bowls or salads
7. Chickpea salad
8. Sheet pan recipes

Dinner:

1. One pot meals
2. Slow cooker meals
3. Freezer meals
4. 30 minute meals
5. Skillet meals

Snacks:

1.Hard-boiled eggs
2. Energy Bites
3. Homemade protein bars
4. Protein powder
5. Beef jerky (low for a lower-sodium, high-quality version; or you can make your own)
6. Veggies & hummus
7. Fruits and nut butters (I'm a sucker for apples and peanut butter)
8. Homemade green smoothies (no yogurt, just fruit/veggies like spinach, celery, apple, lemon, ginger, etc)
9. Nuts like almonds, cashews, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, etc
10. Spiced popcorn
11. Low-sugar, dairy-free fudgsicles
12. Veggie chips

Meal Prep Tips:

1. Keep it simple.
2. Prepare ingredients ahead of time.
3. Use the same ingredients more than once.
4.Keep a well-stocked refridgerator.
5.Keep a well-stocked pantry.
6. Take it easy.
